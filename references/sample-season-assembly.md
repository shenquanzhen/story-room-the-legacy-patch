## Hook Spine Summary
- Opening season hook: a fallen heiress is forced to serve the engagement banquet that replaced her.
- Core suspense question: how much power did Lin Yue really lose, and how many layers of the Gu structure still secretly depend on her?
- Midpoint turn: Lin Yue wins publicly only to discover the original betrayal came from inside her own bloodline.
- Pre-finale collapse: legal constriction, trust fracture, and false-defeat optics make her appear close to losing.
- Final payoff image: the entire hierarchy is forced to recognize Lin Yue as the true power center of the house.

## Premise / Logline
Lin Yue, a disgraced heiress publicly treated as disposable, turns every trap laid by the Gu family into evidence, leverage, and public reversal until the entire power structure that fed on her humiliation is forced to bow.

## Core Cast / Role Notes
- Lin Yue: fallen heiress with hidden legal, financial, and political leverage; emotionally disciplined and strategically patient.
- Gu Nancheng: visible heir and front-facing antagonist whose power relies on borrowed legitimacy.
- Patriarch: highest authority lane whose ambiguity sustains long-range tension.
- Ally lane: useful but dangerous support vector tied to older betrayal architecture and partial truth.
- Family elder / governance / legal blocs: institutional faces of continuity, concealment, and selective legitimacy.
